WHAT IS THE ISLE?

At its foundation 'The Isle' is a player vs player dinosaur survival game, where you can choose between playing as a Herbivore or a Carnivore on a massive remote island. Players will spawn as babies and must grow to adulthood if they wish to survive. There is very little in the way of handholding. Quite simply, its kill or be killed!
