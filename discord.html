ABOUT US

Welcome to The Raptors discord. We are a small, but growing community of like minded gamer's who play as carnivores on The Isle. We pack mostly as Utah's on no specific server but our focus is to meet up, grow and then to encourage others to hunt down prey in coordinated teams. Naturally, we are a PVP orientated community but having fun is our main goal. We take combat seriously and believe everyone has something to teach and something to learn. With this in mind we host two training servers both in the EU and US, see our #our-servers for more info.

SERVER BENEFITS

Grow your confidence and ability Test game aspects and find new hunting/playing strategies Rehearse and refine your pack hunting skills Build teamwork and communication PVP training Tactics training

We are fun and friendly and have players from all skill brackets so we hope you'll stick around and join the growing family!
